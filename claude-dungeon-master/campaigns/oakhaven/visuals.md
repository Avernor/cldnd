# Δρυόχωρο — Visual Reference for AI Generation

Image-prompt-ready descriptions of locations, NPCs, and key objects. Each entry is written to feed directly into Midjourney, DALL·E 3, Stable Diffusion, or similar. Lift verbatim or adapt to your prompt-engineering preference.

## Campaign-wide style anchors

Use these in any prompt to keep visual consistency:

- **Style**: dark fantasy oil painting, painterly realism, low saturation, warm candlelight against cold blue-grey daylight
- **Palette**: charcoal grey, moss green, wet brown, dirty cream, accent of fire-orange and tarnished silver
- **Mood**: late autumn in a wet logging valley; smoke, breath in cold air, lichen on stone
- **References to invoke**: *The Witcher 3 White Orchard*, *Bloodborne Hemwick*, Andrew Wyeth interiors, Caravaggio chiaroscuro for portraits, John Atkinson Grimshaw for streets at dusk
- **Avoid**: bright colors, polished armor, anime style, modern lighting, clean clothes

**Sample prompt wrapper** (Midjourney / DALL·E):
> *[description], dark fantasy oil painting, painterly realism, low saturation, warm candlelight against cold blue-grey daylight, late autumn, smoke and damp, in the style of Andrew Wyeth and John Atkinson Grimshaw, cinematic composition, --ar 3:2 --style raw*

For portraits add: *waist-up portrait, three-quarter view, eyes catching candlelight, oil on canvas texture*
For locations add: *wide establishing shot, atmospheric perspective, golden hour or overcast dusk, no people in frame*

# OBJECTS

## Η Πυξίδα — The Compass

A small brass-cased compass, palm-sized, the casing dark with age and warm to the touch. The face is yellowed ivory marked with **Greek letters instead of compass points** — α, β, γ, δ around the rim — and a needle of dark steel that does not point north but drifts slowly toward nothing in particular. The glass is slightly warped, an old hand-poured pane. Hairline cracks run across the casing where it was once dropped and never repaired. Tiny inlaid silver inside the rim catches firelight when held near a candle. A short braided leather cord hangs from the ring at the top, frayed at the loop. The whole object is the size of a pocket watch but heavier than it looks. **Always faintly warm**, even on a cold day.

**Prompt seed**: *antique brass pocket compass with ivory face, Greek letters α β γ δ around the rim, dark steel needle, warped hand-poured glass, hairline cracks in the brass casing, tarnished silver inlay catching firelight, frayed leather cord, lying on dark wooden tavern table, single candle flame nearby, macro shot, shallow depth of field, oil painting realism*

## Το Ανάποδο Ρολόι — The Backward Watch

A round steel pocket watch, perfectly preserved, found on an ancient skeleton. The face is white enamel cracked into a fine spiderweb of hairlines but unyellowed. The numerals are Roman but **arranged counterclockwise** — XII at the bottom, VI at the top. The hour and minute hands move backward at the correct speed. The second hand is missing. The case has no visible maker's mark, only a small engraved spiral on the back. Heavy for its size. Cold to the touch even when held.

**Prompt seed**: *antique steel pocket watch, white enamel face cracked with fine spiderweb hairlines, Roman numerals arranged counterclockwise XII at the bottom, hands moving backward, lying on a dwarven skeleton's exposed ribs in a dark mine, lantern light from above, dust motes, oil painting realism*

## Το Γράμμα — The Letter

A folded piece of cream-colored parchment, slightly yellowed at the edges, sealed with **dark red wax bearing a dwarven clan sigil** — a stylized hammer crossed with a peak. The fold is sharp and recent. The address on the outside is written in the bard's own hand: *«Στους ταξιδιώτες με την πυξίδα»* (To the travelers with the compass). The interior holds five Greek words: *«Ελάτε στο Δρυόχωρο, ο χρόνος σπάει.»*

**Prompt seed**: *folded cream parchment letter sealed with dark red wax, dwarven clan sigil hammer crossed with mountain peak, sharp recent fold, calligraphic Greek handwriting on the outside, lying on a dark inn table beside a tankard, candlelight, oil painting realism*

# LOCATIONS

## Δρυόχωρο — Village Panorama

A logging village clinging to the steep sides of a narrow river valley. Two-story timber houses with **moss-dark shingled roofs**, smoke from every chimney, lanterns just being lit in windows as dusk falls. A black river — the Marrow — cuts through the center, turning a wooden mill wheel. Pine forest closes in on three sides. To the north, **nine jagged mountain peaks** form a crown above the valley. On a low hill above the village, a ring of **nine standing stones** silhouetted against the sky. The first snow dusts the high pass. Late autumn light, blue-grey overcast.

**Prompt seed**: *wide establishing shot of a small logging village in a narrow river valley at dusk, two-story timber houses with moss-dark shingled roofs, smoke from chimneys, black river through the center turning a wooden mill wheel, dense pine forest on three sides, nine jagged mountain peaks crowning the north horizon, ring of nine standing stones on a hill above the village, late autumn, first snow on high ground, lanterns lit in windows, blue-grey overcast, atmospheric perspective, dark fantasy oil painting --ar 3:2*

## Tips for using these

- **One image per session is plenty.** A panorama of the village to set Scene 1, a portrait of an NPC the party is about to meet, the compass for the table — that's three. Don't drown in art.
- **Generate 2–3 variants per prompt and pick the one closest to your mental image.** AI generators are inconsistent.
- **Save the seeds for any image you love** so you can generate consistent follow-ups in the same style.
- **For cards/handouts**: portraits work best at 3:4 aspect ratio (the default for most generators). Locations work best at 16:9 or 3:2.
- **For Midjourney specifically**: append `--style raw` to suppress overly stylized output. The descriptions above already include this.
