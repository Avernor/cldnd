# DM Notes — The Return of the Fourth
*(Companion to `briefing-returning-player.md` and `recap-opening.md`. English, DM-only.)*

## Settled design
- **Mechanism**: authorless storm-slip. During the unnatural storm, a variable **nobody** authored — not Vanya, not her predecessor — traded him into an adjacent version of this month. Vanya has no log entry for him, no explanation. He is the first thing in the campaign **she** cannot explain. When a future fragment meets him, play her reaction as fascination edged with alarm — an anomaly outside her model.
- **In the real timeline**: he was never on the road. The party has always been three. Their forgetting is rules-legal: he was taken *before* they entered the field at the tower, and the compass only protects memories from edits after exposure. (When the party works this out, let them sit with the implication: *we were edited before we ever found the compass. What else was taken?* Νεφέλη's Session 2 line — «Έχεις ένα λεπτό σκίσιμο στη μνήμη σου» — is now retroactive foreshadowing. She felt the scar he left.)
- **Entry**: cold open. Read the recap; two-second beat; he speaks from inside the marching order — «Λοιπόν; Ποιος μπαίνει πρώτος;» — as if always present. In his memory, the four of them solved the door together seconds ago. Play the players' real double-takes as the scene.
- **Character**: [ΟΝΟΜΑ], Fighter 3 (Echo Knight), milestone track. In his memory he was always a fighter and has known the PCs for years. (Out-of-fiction he was going to be a sorcerer — see optional seed below.)

## The faceless echo
His Manifest Echo is a gray, translucent duplicate **without a face** — deliberately rhyming with τα χωρίς πρόσωπα. He finds this normal. Nobody else will.
- If the party meets the Session 4 road Άφτιαχτο *before* his first in-combat manifest, the moment he summons the echo afterward should chill the table: his ability looks like the monster.
- In his briefing, Κόρακας recoiled from the echo («Κράτα το αυτό. Μακριά από μένα.») — a thread you can pay off whenever the real Κόρακας finally meets him.
- Long-term question you never have to answer soon: is the echo an alternate him, a draft him — or something wearing the shape of one?

## Verification toolkit (cap the "you weren't there" argument at one scene)
- **Λυχνία της Βάνια** pointed at him: he **shimmers** (edited/touched by the thin spot). First real use of the lens.
- **Κέλης's ledger** — his own briefing tells him to trust it («ρώτα το κατάστιχο του Κέλη»), so *he* will propose the test that judges him. DM dial: the clean answer is no fourth name; the cruel-good answer is one smudged margin entry in handwriting Κέλης doesn't remember using.
- **Νεφέλη** takes his wrist, the way she took theirs: *«Τώρα ξέρω τι έλειπε.»*

## Dials — RESOLVED (DM decisions)
1. **Stones**: ✅ **He counts NINE, normally — the others still can't.** He can perceive the hidden Watcher. Beat scripted in [README.md](./README.md) Scene 6C. Long-term: he is now the party's only eye on the Ninth Stone thread — when the time comes, he is the one who can find her, describe her, speak to her.
2. **Anchoring**: ✅ **Fully anchored.** No flicker, no tower-sewing. He is simply, impossibly, stable — which should worry Vanya more, not less («Ποιος σε έφτιαξε εσένα;»).
3. **Optional seed — the sorcerer that never was**: still open; rare dreams where his hands move through gestures he never learned. Use only if you want a slow-burn thread about the *other* version of him. Never explain.
4. **Milestone timing**: recommended in README — all four to level 4 at the END of Session 4, before the tower.

## Table logistics
- Send him the briefing privately; tell him to guard it and play his memories as plain truth. His confusion is real; so is theirs — nobody has to act.
- Check he's comfortable spending his first session back being disbelieved. Give him the OOC assurance that the party *will* get proof (the toolkit above) within the session.
- The three players' genuine lack of shared history with him **is** the special effect. Don't over-engineer; let the table do it.
- Fill in the [bracketed] name slots and adjust the three bond-memories in his briefing (whetstone / clan-dead names / the song he never plays) to match the actual PC backstories before sending.
