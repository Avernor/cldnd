## 5A — Ο Κόρακας *(The Rook)*

**Where**: His cabin, ~2 hours northeast of the village, at the edge of Ο Παλαιόδρυμος. Trail-finding required.

**Who**: Hunter, 40s. Lives alone. Has seen Vanya "in two places at once." Does not go into the deep woods anymore.

**What he gives if approached**:
- Confirms a hooded woman appeared near the village twice — *at the same time* — about two weeks ago
- Names what's in the woods: *«τα χωρίς πρόσωπα»*
- **Six iron nails**: one-use each, gives an Άφτιαχτο disadvantage on its next attack. Hand-forged, simple, on a leather thong. *«Παλιό κόλπο. Ξέρω άντρες που λένε ότι λειτουργεί.»*
- His silence about the deep woods. He won't explain why he no longer goes — *«Δεν είναι κουβέντα για ανθρώπους που έχουν δουλειά να κάνουν.»* If pressed hard with Persuasion DC 16, he says: *«Είδα ένα πράγμα μ' το πρόσωπο της κόρης του γείτονα. Η κόρη του γείτονα δεν υπάρχει. Δεν υπήρξε ποτέ. Αυτό το ξέρω. Αλλά εγώ την είδα. Δεν θέλω να ξαναδώ.»*

**Time cost**: 4-5 hours round trip. **Probably skip this session.** Save for Session 4.

**Read-aloud (Greek) — arrival at his cabin**

> Η καλύβα είναι μικρή και χτισμένη ανάμεσα σε δύο μεγάλα έλατα. Ο καπνός που βγαίνει απ' το φουγάρο μυρίζει αρκάδι και κάτι άλλο — τομάρι που στεγνώνει. Πάνω στην πόρτα, καρφωμένο μ' ένα παλιό σιδερένιο καρφί, ένα μικρό σύμβολο σχηματισμένο από κλαδάκια. Δεν αναγνωρίζετε το σχέδιο.

## 5B — Κάλχας Βόσσος *(The Mill Owner / The Journals)*

**Where**: Ο Μύλος του Βόσσου or his residence next to it.

**Who**: Mill owner, hard, suspicious of outsiders. Economic power of the village. Quietly hates change.

**The journals**: His grandfather's diaries, three volumes, kept in a locked chest in the residence study. Cover ~50 years of village observations including the *Ήσυχες Μέρες* (Quiet Days). **Hardest thread to access.**

**Three avenues**:
- **Trust** — earn it through service to the village (rescuing Θωμάς already counts as a small deposit; saving Ορέστης or Μίρα would deepen). DC 18 Persuasion when asking.
- **Theft** — DC 16 Stealth + DC 15 thieves' tools on the chest. Loud failure brings the foreman.
- **Sister Αλκμήνη** — she has access. If the Fighter brings her his heirloom sword, she will recognize the forging style. If they then ask her about the journals, DC 14 Persuasion: she can borrow them quietly *for one night*. They must be returned.

**What's in the journals (if accessed)**:
- The grandfather records *«Ήσυχη Μέρα»* events: 4 in his early decades, 8 in his middle decades, 19 in his last decade alive. **Acceleration is documented.**
- A page about Αριστόδημος Κρέων — *«ο νέος απ' την Ακαδημία»* — who asked too many questions and *«εξαφανίστηκε»* (vanished) before answering any
- **A torn-out page**. Someone has interfered with the journals. The torn page is missing. If they search the chest carefully (DC 15 Investigation), they find the page hidden under the lining — a single line: *«Δεν είναι μία γυναίκα. Είναι πολλές που μοιάζουν.»* — written in handwriting that is **not the grandfather's.** Someone else is reading these journals.

**Time cost**: 1-2 hours.

## 5C — Γερόντισσα Εστία *(The Matriarch / The Names of the Watchers)*

**Where**: Her home near the chapel, mornings she sits outside; afternoons she is inside.

**Who**: Village matriarch, 70s. Sharp memory for old things, confused about recent ones (early thin-spot symptom). Knows the names of all Nine Watchers in a forgotten language.

**What she gives if approached**:
- Confirms there are nine stones. Insists on it. *«Εγώ ήμουν παιδί όταν τις μέτρησα. Εννιά ήταν. Εννιά είναι.»*
- Will recite the **first eight names** of the Watchers in the old tongue without hesitation. **Cannot remember the ninth.** Tries. Stops. Looks at her hands. *«Ήξερα. Ήξερα και την ένατη. Δεν θυμάμαι.»*
- Hums the same wordless tune Ιώ hums (without coordinating). **If the party brings Ιώ here**, the two of them will hum together for almost a minute, eyes closed, neither aware. *Major atmosphere beat.*
- A small wooden charm she will give them — a knot of woven hair tied to a piece of yew. *«Πάρτε το. Δεν ξέρω τι κάνει. Η μάνα μου μου το έδωσε. Δεν λειτούργησε για μένα. Ίσως για σας.»* (Free flavor item — give it +1 to a single Wis save against thin-spot effects, one use. Don't tell the players.)

**Time cost**: 30 min - 1 hour.

## 5D — Θάλεια *(The Widow Who Doesn't Know)*

**Where**: A small back room of Τα Είδη της Μαρίνας. Works shifts washing linen. Easiest to find at midday.

**Who**: A mill-hand's widow who does not know she is a widow. Her husband Άνδρος was Vanya's earliest erasure experiment — fully unmade. She remembers nothing about him. She dreams of a man she cannot describe.

**Why this thread connects to Session 3**:
If the Άφτιαχτο in Thread 4 named itself as **Άνδρος**, the party now carries a message from him: *«Πες της ότι δεν ήταν λάθος της.»* They have to decide whether, when, and how to tell her.

**This is not a puzzle. It is a character moment.** Don't make it transactional.

**If they meet her cold (without the Άφτιαχτο grace note)**:
- She is friendly, tired, unremarkable
- She flinches if anyone says *Άνδρος* casually. Does not know why
- She has a man's wool shirt in her drawers. Wrong size. Tells herself it was her father's. It wasn't
- She drinks tea from a chipped wooden cup carved with a lumberjack's guild mark
- **Νεφέλη and Marina both quietly worry about her.** Neither will explain why

**If they meet her with Άνδρος's message**:
- She will not believe them at first
- The wool shirt is what breaks her. If a PC suggests she look at it, smell it, hold it: she will start crying without knowing why
- Eventually she will accept the message. Not gladly. With confusion and grief. *«Δεν ξέρω αυτόν τον άνθρωπο. Αλλά ξέρω αυτό που μου είπες. Νιώθω ότι είναι αληθινό. Πώς μπορώ να θρηνήσω κάποιον που δεν θυμάμαι;»*
- She will not become an ally. She will become someone the party *carries.* The campaign is heavier from this moment forward.

**Νεφέλη's role here**: she will sit with Θάλεια afterward. *«Άσε με μ' αυτήν. Δεν είναι δουλειά για ξένους.»* The party can leave knowing she is held.

**XP for delivering the message with care**: 200 XP per PC, Inspiration to the speaker. **Probably the highest emotional payoff of the session if it lands.**

**Time cost**: 30 min - 1 hour for the conversation.
