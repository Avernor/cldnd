## Scene 5 — Νεφέλη, deep night (expanded)

She opens the door, sees **four**, goes very still. Then, in order:

1. **Θωμάς is awake by the fire** (he lives here). He looks at the 4th for a long moment, then says, matter-of-fact:
   > *«Εσάς τους τρεις σας βλέπω διπλούς τώρα. Όλους. Όλο το χωριό. Δύο εικόνες, λίγο ξεχειλωμένες, η μία πάνω στην άλλη.»* — κοιτάζει τον [ΟΝΟΜΑ] — *«Εσένα σε βλέπω **μονό.** Είσαι ο μόνος μονός άνθρωπος που έχω δει από τότε που βγήκα απ' το ορυχείο. Ξεκουράζεις τα μάτια μου.»*
   
   *(The fully-anchored dial made diegetic: the impossible man is the most REAL thing in the village. Let that sit.)*
2. **The skeleton under its white sheet** is on her table. The 4th stops dead: *«Τον φέρατε; Εμείς τον αφήσαμε στα Σπλάχνα...»* — divergence #5 fires itself.
3. **The wrist**: *«Το σκίσιμο στη μνήμη σας… Τώρα ξέρω τι έλειπε.»* **The lens**: he shimmers; so, faintly, do all four.
4. **The needle-turn** (the un-played Scene 2 of session-03): she turns the Fighter's hand; the compass strikes — the needle has left the Reyne house and points **north**, steady, at the pass. *«Α. Εκεί λοιπόν.»*
5. **The clinical reading**: she makes tea nobody asked for and has them read TWO items tonight — the **experiment log** and the **Δρυόχωρο edit-map** (thread-6 texts). Cold, numbered, factual: Σίλας, Ηλίας Μαρίνου, Άνδρος Πετρόπουλος, the vessels, *«τα Άφτιαχτα είναι απορρίμματα»*. The party goes to sleep with a list of names. **Tomorrow, the day will prove every one of them true.** Νεφέλη's only comment: *«Αυτή η γυναίκα δεν θα σταματήσει.»*

## Scene 6 — Morning: the inn, Τα Χελιδόνια, Κυρα-Ασπασία

Run the arrival from [guests-troupe.md](./guests-troupe.md) — the painted cart, the corner table (Vanya's old table), the village remembering it has a fair in ten days. **Κυρα-Ασπασία** works the room (full NPC sheet in guests file): she books the fair with the Elders, needles Αλέξης about winter lodging, reads the party in one pass — and then runs **Η Μοιρασιά** (guests file): she assigns **one Swallow to each PC for the day** (*«Μία στον καθένα, κορίτσια. Πετάξτε.»*). From this moment every scene the party enters, their paired guest enters too — fortune-teller↔the 4th, singer↔Bard, dancer↔Fighter, companion↔Paladin. Her job-assignments remain the backup tool if anyone drifts idle (*«Μάθε μου ποιος κουμαντάρει το πανηγύρι. Εσύ, τον παπά — γιατί δεν χτύπησε καμπάνα χθες;»*).

- **The stag head**: someone from his briefing angle — the 4th glances at the hearth. One antler. *«Ποιος του έσπασε το κέρατο;»* Divergence #2, free.
- **Αλέξης's line** (thread-3 hook, verbatim): *«Ο Ορέστης χθες βράδυ... δεν ήταν καλά. Έλεγε πράγματα. Σήμερα δεν έχει ανοίξει το παρεκκλήσι. Δεν είναι του χαρακτήρα του.»* — the bell has not rung for the first time in fourteen years. Plants menu item A.
- **Ασπασία's senior moment** *(the dial — drop it casually here, pay it off after the Reading)*: she played this same fair ~35 years ago, and danced at a village wedding that season. *«Θυμάμαι τη νύφη — κοριτσάκι απ' το μαγαζί, όλο γέλιο. Θυμάμαι το γέλιο του γαμπρού. Το πρόσωπό του — τίποτα. Λες και χόρεψα με κενό. Γερνάω, κορίτσια.»* She thinks it's age. The party, holding a log that says *«Πειραματισμός 2 — Ηλίας Μαρίνου... η σύζυγος κράτησε σώμα και χρήμα, έχασε μνήμη»*, knows better. If they say the name **Ηλίας** to her: she goes white, and a *piece* returns — the laugh gets a jaw, a scar above an eyebrow, never the whole face. **The first out-of-valley witness. Μαρίνα's ring now has a wedding attached to it.** Whether they take this proof to Μαρίνα (lore.md — gently, the ring, the ledger) is their choice; Νεφέλη counsels mercy.

## Scene 7 — Combat 2: Το Πράγμα στον Στάβλο *(fires by itself, mid-morning)*

**Trigger**: screaming — not human. The troupe's cart-horses, stabled at **Ο Στάβλος του Κέλη** at the Crossing end (the village's wild edge — thread-4's domestic-space rule respected). Half the village runs. The party arrives to find Κέλης white-knuckled on a pitchfork outside his own paddock, and inside it —

### Read-aloud (Greek)

> Το πράγμα στέκεται ανάμεσα στα άλογα, που έχουν στριμωχτεί όλα στην ίδια γωνιά με τα μάτια γυρισμένα άσπρα. Δεν τους επιτίθεται. Στέκεται πολύ κοντά τους, με το κεφάλι γερμένο — σαν να τους **ρωτάει κάτι.**
>
> Φοράει ό,τι απόμεινε από ρούχα δουλευτή. Το πρόσωπό του κουνιέται — ψάχνει σε ποιανού πρόσωπο μοιάζει σήμερα.
>
> Γυρίζει προς το μέρος σας. Και με μια φωνή που δεν είναι δική του και δεν είναι μία, λέει:
>
> *«...ξέρω εσένα...»*

**It followed the cart in.** It has been pacing the road for days — the Χελιδόνια were the last warm things moving through its woods, and it walked behind their music all the way to the valley. (The dancer's card suddenly has a second meaning: something was on that road besides the hooded woman.)

**Run it from [thread-4](../session-03/thread-4-aftiakto.md)** with these adjustments:
- **HP 40** (four PCs now). Everything else per the block — psychic touch, Wisdom drain ("you suddenly cannot remember your father's voice — it comes back"), Take What's Missing on a 5-6 recharge, compass aura, radiant vulnerability.
- **Voice slips SET B** (guests file) — the creature speaks through the guests, one line at a time, on your point.
- **Guests in the scene**: behind the fence with Ασπασία, who has produced a knife from somewhere and stands in front of her girls. Εύνοια tokens live. Non-combatants — but a guest *naming what she sees* out loud is free horror.
- **The grace note**: they READ the log last night. They have the name. If a PC channels healing/inspiration and speaks **«Άνδρος»** — run thread-4's restoration verbatim: the carpenter's hands, pine and river, *«Πες της ότι δεν ήταν λάθος της.»* — and Πειραματισμός 3 stands proven in front of the whole village. **This is the session's loudest answer.** If they fight instead: it fades at 0 HP, and something is taken anyway.
- **After-beat**: the compass cools — *«σαν να αναπνέει με ανακούφιση».*

**Aftermath — the ledger, unprompted**: Κέλης, shaken, owes them. He brings the ledger out himself:
- The troupe's arrival, fresh ink, yesterday's date. Mundane, verifiable.
- **Στέφανος Παπαϊωάννου** — registered alive and working *the same year* his gravestone says he drowned (thread-3's contradiction, now witnessed on paper).
- **Άνδρος Πετρόπουλος** — marriage entry, mill roster, then nothing. A man the paper remembers and the village doesn't.
- **The 4th**: your chosen dial — clean absence, or the smudged margin line. Either way: *«Εσένα δεν σε έγραψα να μπαίνεις. Και εγώ γράφω τους πάντες.»*

**Θάλεια (conditional — be ready)**: if Άνδρος was named in front of villagers, word reaches Μαρίνα's shop within the hour. The party carries a dead man's message. Run 5D **only if they pursue it today** — midday, the back room, the wool shirt; Νεφέλη takes over after (*«Άσε με μ' αυτήν. Δεν είναι δουλειά για ξένους.»*). Otherwise it keeps for Session 5+. Do not force it into an overfull day.

## Scene 8 — Menu of two

*(If the party splits, the paired Swallows split with them — Μοιρασιά rule. The Μάρθα payoff needs the **companion** at the burial; she's paired with the Paladin, and if he picks the stones, Ασπασία redirects her: «Εσύ — στην κηδεία.»)*

**A. Το Παρεκκλήσι — Ορέστης and the burial.** Run thread-3 exactly: the back room, the sour wine, the folded hood, **the clean knife** (don't point at it; reward the player who sees it). Get him up — water, a song, a hand. Then give him work: the two vessels need graves. He recognizes **Στέφανος** from his own list; if the guests attend, the companion names **κυρα-Μάρθα**. Two real burials, two real names, the bell rings again before dusk — *«Δεν ήξερα ποιον θα καλούσε. Τώρα ξέρω. Αυτούς τους δύο.»* He gives the party **the eight-name list** from the registry drawer. *(He is saved, the list is in play, and the crack-in-public timeline is defused.)*

**B. Οι Εννιά Φύλακες — the count.** As previously scripted: three count eight and get the headache; the 4th counts nine, shrugs, points — *«Στη βάση της έχει φρέσκο ψωμί. Ποιος αφήνει προσφορά σε πέτρα που δεν βλέπει;»* Optional bridge: fetch **Γερόντισσα Εστία** (5C) — she insists on nine (*«Εγώ ήμουν παιδί όταν τις μέτρησα. Εννιά ήταν. Εννιά είναι.»*), recites eight Watcher names in the old tongue, and cannot remember precisely the ninth. She and the 4th look at each other like the only two sane people in the valley. Her yew charm goes to whoever she likes best. Dread dial as before: the first time he *describes* the ninth aloud, the air on the hill changes.

## Scene 9 — The Reading (her heart)

Late afternoon, Νεφέλη's table, everything laid out: the **working notebook** (she knows them; she cannot touch them while they hold the compass; Μίρα is the way in), the **personal journal** (Ο Δάσκαλος; the dead boy; *«Δεν θα είναι κλοπή. Θα είναι αγάπη.»*), the **regional map** (Καίλεν, and the small dot marked **«Η Πληγή»**), the **unsent letter to her dead child**. If the singer chooses this moment for «Η Μάνα κι οι Ώρες», do not stop her. Νεφέλη's independent verdict: *«Φοβάμαι ότι αυτό που ψάχνετε δεν είναι μόνο μία γυναίκα.»*

**The interlock beat**: someone will put **Ορέστης's list** next to **her log**. Eight graves; five experiments; they key into each other like teeth. Let the players do the matching themselves — say nothing, refill the tea. This is the discover-it-with-their-own-hands moment the session was built for.

## Scene 10 — Dusk: probe II

Thread-2 mechanics, party in the room, and **rougher than before** — she is rattled tonight (she saw a fourth). If a heavy result lands with Νεφέλη present, use the foreshadow line: she catches *«Δικό μου το παιδί. Δικό μου ήταν πάντα»* and repeats it, shaken. Close on her limit: *«Δεν μπορώ να το κάνω αυτό κάθε σούρουπο... Η βελόνα σας ξέρει πού πρέπει να πάτε. **Τελειώστε το.**»*

**The compass dilemma is now live for S5** (thread-2): take it north, or leave it shielding Μίρα. Let them argue. Don't resolve it tonight.

## Scene 11 — Dawn departure

As previously written: garlands going up, the Χελιδόνια asleep behind painted curtains, four shadows long in the snow. **Level everyone to 4.**
