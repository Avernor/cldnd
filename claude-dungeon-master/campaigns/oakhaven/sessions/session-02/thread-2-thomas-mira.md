# Thread 2 — Η Μίρα Έρχεται για τον Θωμά
*(Mira Comes for Thomas)*

> **Premise**: Θωμάς has been "seeing double" since the party rescued him from the mine in Session 1. His exposure to the thin spot at depth made him *permeable* to edits — he is the first non-compass witness who can perceive what Vanya does. Today his condition deteriorates fast, and his pregnant wife Μίρα — frightened, sleepless, alone — comes to the inn to ask for help. **This is the only thread with real in-world harm for neglect.**

**Entry**: Μίρα arrives at the inn mid-morning during Scene 1.

---

## NPCs in this thread

### 🔑 Μίρα Τεφρή (Mira Ash) — pregnant, sharp, the party's first friend

**Description (read aloud)**:
> Μια γυναίκα στα τέλη της εικοσαετίας της, ορατά έγκυος — γύρω στους έξι μήνες. Καστανοκόκκινα μαλλιά πλεγμένα πάνω απ' τον έναν ώμο. Έντονα πράσινα μάτια, φακίδες στη μύτη και στα ζυγωματικά. Φοράει ένα πρακτικό σκούρο μάλλινο φόρεμα, μια λινή ποδιά, μια μάλλινη σάλι σφιχτά τυλιγμένη γύρω απ' τους ώμους. Τα χέρια της κόκκινα και σκασμένα απ' το πλύσιμο. Χλωμή, αδυνατισμένη στο πρόσωπο — η κούραση κάποιας που πέρασε νύχτα σχεδόν χωρίς ύπνο, με φόβο πάνω στην εγκυμοσύνη της. Στέκει πολύ ίσια ακόμα κι όταν φοβάται. Στον λαιμό της, μια απλή ασημένια αλυσίδα κρατάει τη βέρα του γάμου της.

**Voice (read aloud as needed)**: low, even, sometimes near-whispered. Polite to a fault when frightened. Direct when calm.
- *«Συγγνώμη που σας ενοχλώ τόσο νωρίς.»*
- *«Ο Θωμάς... δεν είναι καλά. Από τη νύχτα που τον φέρατε πίσω.»*
- *«Δεν ξέρω σε ποιον άλλον να μιλήσω.»*
- *«Σας παρακαλώ. Αν μπορείτε.»*
- *«Δεν είμαι τρελή. Κάτι δεν πάει καλά εδώ. Εδώ και χρόνια. Το χωριό απλώς αρνείται να το πει δυνατά.»*

**Goals (this session)**:
- Get Θωμάς out of bed. Get him to recognize her.
- Keep her unborn child safe.
- Find people she can trust.

**Knows**:
- Θωμάς is not himself since the rescue. He drifts, forgets her name for seconds at a time, sees things over her shoulder she cannot see.
- The party rescued him once; she believes they can again.
- That she is about a week away from feeling truly afraid for the baby. She has not let herself feel it yet.

**Doesn't know**:
- That Vanya's fragment is what is hurting Θωμάς (and that the same fragment is now eyeing her pregnancy).
- That she is Κάλχας Βόσσος's cousin by marriage and a word from her opens his door (Thread 8).

**Family connection**: Μίρα is **Κάλχας Βόσσος's cousin by marriage** — her mother was sister to Κάλχας's father. They are not close, but the blood is real, and Κάλχας respects her judgment. **A handwritten note from Μίρα drops the DC for Thread 8 from 16 to 11.**

**If the party helps**: she gives them total quiet trust, sends them to Κάλχας with a note, will believe them when they later tell her something hard.

**If the party refuses or delays**: she goes home. By dusk she comes back once more, dry-eyed and terrible. After that, no second chances. She will not trust them with anything ever again — and Vanya's eye is now on her child.

### 🔑 Θωμάς Τεφρός (Thomas Ash) — mill worker, edit-permeable

**Description (read aloud)**:
> Άντρας στα τέλη της εικοσαετίας του, λεπτός και ηλιοκαμένος όπως όλοι οι εργάτες του μύλου. Καστανά μαλλιά κομμένα κοντά. Μια λεπτή ουλή κόβει τη γέφυρα της μύτης του, από ένα παλιό ατύχημα στα ξύλα. Φοράει ένα τραχύ λινό πουκάμισο, δερμάτινο γιλέκο, μάλλινα παντελόνια, γερές μπότες. Τα χέρια του βαριά καλιαρισμένα, κόκκινα κι ωμά απ' το κρύο. **Από τη νύχτα της διάσωσης απ' το ορυχείο, τα μάτια του έχουν ένα μακρινό, σχεδόν διπλοεστιασμένο βλέμμα** — σαν να βλέπει πάντα κάτι λίγο πίσω απ' τον ώμο όποιου του μιλά. Αξύριστος. Σκοτεινούς κύκλους. Μια απλή πλεγμένη γαμήλια κορδέλα γύρω απ' τον έναν καρπό.

**Voice (read aloud as needed)**: slow, halting, unsure. Catches his own thread mid-sentence and loses it again.
- *«Είστε... ήρθατε για το τσάι;»*
- *«Περιμένω κάποιον. Ξέρω πως περιμένω κάποιον. Αλλά δεν θυμάμαι το όνομα.»*
- *«Μίρα... ποια είσαι;»*
- *«Ο Σίλας; Τον είδα χθες βράδυ.»* *(with the compass present)*
- *«Με σπρώχνει έξω. Κάτι με σπρώχνει έξω απ' το σπίτι μου.»*

**Goals**: Hold onto himself. Be Mira's husband. Remember his own name. He does not realize how fast he is losing the war.

**Knows (with the compass present, within 10 ft)**:
- The mine, *correctly*. Not just the rescue — the things he saw down there in the dark while he was alone. **Ask him about the deeper levels and he will describe them in detail he should not have.** A dim memory of *something else down there besides the skeleton* — a low hum, a hand-shape on a wall, a tunnel that "wasn't supposed to be open." (DM: this is foreshadowing for Session 4+ — there is more in Τα Σπλάχνα the party did not find.)
- Σίλας alive two nights ago. He can hold both the alive memory and the "ten years dead" memory simultaneously, and the dissonance is physically painful.
- The double-vision: he describes seeing *two of the same person* sometimes when he looks at a villager. The "extra one" is faintly there and faintly wrong. (DM: he is seeing remnants of edited versions of people. The first PC who is *not* the bearer to walk in front of him, he sees as a ghostly second self for a moment.)

**Knows (without the compass)**:
- The altered timeline only. Σίλας dead ten years. Mira is his wife. He loves her. He cannot remember why he is so tired.

**The deterioration clock** (full table below) is what makes this thread mechanical — Θωμάς gets worse hour by hour if not addressed. The compass *holds* his decline as long as it stays within 10 ft of him.

### 🔑 Γριά Νεφέλη (Old Nefeli) — midwife, may surface in this thread

**Description (read aloud)**:
> Γριά γύρω στα εξήντα-κάτι, μικρή, λεπτή σαν χειμωνιάτικο σύρμα. Σιδερόγκριζα μαλλιά πιασμένα σε σφιχτό κότσο, με μια κλωστή πάντα να ξεφεύγει. Χλωμά, υδαρή γαλάζια μάτια που δεν χάνουν τίποτα. Φοράει πολλαπλές στρώσεις από γκρίζο και καφέ μαλλί — φούστα, φόρεμα, πλεκτή σάλι, όλα φτιαγμένα στο χέρι κι επιδιορθωμένα πολλές φορές. Τα χέρια της λεκιασμένα πράσινα απ' τα βότανα. Στη ζώνη της, ένα μικρό δερμάτινο πουγκί με ξερά φυτά. Αργές, ακριβείς κινήσεις. Δύο γάτες πλέκονται στα πόδια της. Ένα κλαδί αποξηραμένου δεντρολίβανου χωμένο πίσω απ' το ένα της αυτί.

**Voice (read aloud as needed)**: slow, matter-of-fact, pauses mid-sentence to look at things other people don't see. Addresses everyone as *«παιδί μου»*. Becomes *quieter* when worried, not louder.
- *«Κάθισε, παιδί μου. Τσάι. Όχι, δεν σε ρώτησα αν θέλεις — θα πιεις τσάι.»*
- *«Υπάρχουν πράγματα που δεν είναι άρρωστα. Είναι απλώς... λάθος. Καταλαβαίνεις τη διαφορά;»*
- *«Το χωριό μου μιλάει περισσότερο απ' ό,τι νομίζει ο καθένας. Ακούω.»*
- *(Looking at the compass without being shown it)*: *«Κρύβεις κάτι ζεστό στην τσέπη σου. Όχι, μην το βγάλεις. Εγώ το είπα, εσύ δεν είπες τίποτα.»*

**Goals**: Tend the village. Deliver Mira's baby. Notice what others do not.

**What she will notice during this thread**: she is the village's first early-warning system for the pregnancy. If the party visits her during this session, choose **one** of these for her to surface (do not use all three):

1. **The thyme bundle.** A dried bundle of thyme over her hearth begins to turn slowly when the compass enters the room. She looks at it. She looks at the party. She says nothing.
2. **The pregnancy.** *«Η Μίρα Τεφρή κουβαλάει ένα κοριτσάκι. Το ξέρω από την όγδοη εβδομάδα. Αλλά τις τελευταίες μέρες... η αίσθηση κουνιέται. Σαν να μη θέλει να κάτσει ήσυχη. Δεν μου αρέσει αυτό.»* — the first hint that Vanya may already have her eye on the pregnancy.
3. **Reading a PC's wrist.** If a PC accepts her tea, she takes their wrist (not their hand) and holds it for a moment. *«Έχεις ένα λεπτό σκίσιμο στη μνήμη σου. Μικρό ακόμη. Πρόσεχε.»* — she senses compass-exposure on them.

**She will not directly help with Θωμάς's condition** — her hedge-knowledge cannot fix what is wrong with him. But she will say, gently: *«Δεν είναι άρρωστος, παιδί μου. Είναι... μετακινημένος. Δεν έχω βότανο γι' αυτό.»* Useful diagnostic if the party hasn't connected it themselves.

**Her cottage** (read aloud if visited):
> Ένα μικρό, μονόχωρο πέτρινο σπίτι. Δέσμες στεγνωμένων βοτάνων κρέμονται από κάθε δοκάρι. Δύο γάτες κοιμούνται σε διαφορετικές γωνιές. Μια χαμηλή φωτιά στο τζάκι. Δίπλα της, μια κουνιστή πολυθρόνα με μια διπλωμένη σάλι. Μυρίζει στεγνά φυτά και καπνό.

Τα μάτια της έχουν τα σκοτεινά του αϋπνίας. «Συγγνώμη που σας ενοχλώ τόσο νωρίς», λέει ήσυχα. «Ο Θωμάς... δεν είναι καλά. Από τη νύχτα που τον φέρατε πίσω. Σας παρακαλώ, ελάτε μαζί μου. Δεν ξέρω σε ποιον άλλον να μιλήσω.»

She does not push. If the party hesitates, she steps back, says *«Καταλαβαίνω. Συγγνώμη που σας ενόχλησα.»* — and turns to leave. **The party gets one beat to call her back.** If they don't, the deterioration clock starts.

## Branches

### A. Speak with Θωμάς with the compass nearby

The compass warms. Θωμάς leans toward it like a man leaning toward fire in the cold.
- With the compass on the table between them (which is fine — set down ≠ stowed), Θωμάς can hold a thread of memory for ~30 seconds at a time.
- If the party asks him about **the mine**, he *remembers it correctly* for the first time since they rescued him. He describes details he should not know — a humming in the dark below the level the party reached, a hand-shape pressed into rock that looked like a child's, a tunnel the party did not see.
- If they ask him about **Σίλας** — watch his face. He closes his eyes. When he opens them, both memories are in his head at once and the dissonance hurts. *«Τον είδα χθες βράδυ. Μου έδωσε μια μπύρα. Μου είπε ότι ο γιος του Λιβέρη χρωστάει χρήματα και ότι δεν θα τα ξαναδεί. Και τώρα η Μίρα μού λέει ότι τον έθαψαν πριν δέκα χρόνια. Και τα θυμάμαι και τα δύο. Και τα δύο είναι αλήθεια. Τι μου κάνετε;»* He begins to cry quietly. The compass is the only reason he can hold both truths at once.
- If they ask about **the woman in the inn the night they returned** (Vanya), his face does not change. *«Δεν θυμάμαι γυναίκα. Θυμάμαι μόνο εσάς και τον Σίλας και τον Αλέξη και να μην ξέρω ποιος ήταν αλήθεια.»* The compass holds the memory of Σίλας for him; it does not give him a memory of Vanya he never had.

### B. Push him too hard

**Wisdom (Insight) DC 13** to notice he is paying a cost for each attempt. He comes back from each "remembering" paler.

If they push past three or four attempts in quick succession, he faints. **Wisdom (Medicine) DC 12** or any healing spell stabilizes him. On waking, he looks at the party and for ten full seconds does not know who they are. **Μίρα sees this happen.** She will not forgive easily.

### C. Comfort Μίρα

She is unraveling. Persuasion or simply kindness (no check needed — play it out) steadies her.

If the party commits to helping Θωμάς over the coming days, she gives them her quiet, total trust. **This unlocks the *low-DC path* into [thread-8-vossos-journals.md](./thread-8-vossos-journals.md)** — Μίρα writes a small note: *«Εμπιστέψου τους. Η Μίρα.»* She tells them where to find Κάλχας and how to approach him.

If they ask her about the village's strangeness, she will hesitate, then say: *«Δεν είμαι τρελή. Κάτι δεν πάει καλά εδώ. Εδώ και χρόνια. Το χωριό απλώς αρνείται να το πει δυνατά.»* This is the *first time anyone has admitted it to them.* Honor that.

### D. Mention Σίλας to Θωμάς off-compass

If the bearer is not in the room (or has stepped out, or set the compass elsewhere), Θωμάς remembers Σίλας the way everyone else does — dead ten years in a barfight, the tragic widower who lost his daughter Κόρη first. His voice softens.
- *«Ο Σίλας; Ο Θεός να τον έχει καλά. Ήμουν παιδί όταν έγινε ο καβγάς, αλλά το θυμάμαι. Η Μίρα λέει ότι έκλαιγε όλη η πλατεία.»*

No flicker. No dissonance. The altered-timeline memory is what his mind reaches for first.

**The party should feel the difference between this and 2.A as a *mechanic of the compass.* Without it, Θωμάς is just another villager. With it, he is the only other witness who can hold both truths. Make that contrast legible in play.**

### E. Visit Νεφέλη for diagnostic help

If the party wants medical help, Μίρα directs them to Γριά Νεφέλη's cottage (or Γερο-Αλέξης suggested it back at the inn).

See Νεφέλη's NPC profile above for what she notices and what she will say. **Her one diagnostic line if the party asks her about Θωμάς directly**:
> *«Δεν είναι άρρωστος, παιδί μου. Είναι... μετακινημένος. Δεν έχω βότανο γι' αυτό. Αλλά αν τον φέρετε εδώ, θα κάτσω μαζί του. Καμιά φορά αυτό φτάνει.»*

If the party brings Θωμάς to Νεφέλη's cottage with the compass present, Θωμάς will fall asleep within ten minutes — peacefully, for the first time in days. Νεφέλη nods once, says nothing, and stays with him while the party does what they need to do. **This counts as engagement** — Θωμάς's clock pauses while he is here.

## Θωμάς's deterioration clock

Θωμάς degrades whether or not the party visits — but the compass (within 10 ft of him for a sustained period) *holds* his decline. Lock the clock when they are actively with him; resume it when they leave.

| Time | State | Read-aloud hook | Mechanical notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Πρωί (morning) — when Μίρα arrives** | *Permeable.* He recognizes them, loses the thread every ~30 seconds, catches it again. Poured himself tea he doesn't remember pouring. Calm, gentle, frightened. | *«Είστε... ήρθατε για το τσάι;»* | Baseline. No check needed to speak to him. Insight DC 13 notices the cost of each "remembering." |
| **Μεσημέρι (midday) — ~3 hrs later** | *Drifting.* Has started to mistake Μίρα for his mother once or twice and then apologize, horrified. Will not eat. Sits by the window and watches the road as if waiting for someone he cannot name. | *«Περιμένω κάποιον. Ξέρω πως περιμένω κάποιον. Αλλά δεν θυμάμαι το όνομα.»* / *«Με σπρώχνει έξω. Κάτι με σπρώχνει έξω απ' το σπίτι μου.»* | Insight DC 12 (easier — it is plain now). If the party brings the compass he stabilizes to morning levels for as long as it stays. Without it, any conversation costs him **1 level of exhaustion** (no save). |
| **Απόγευμα (afternoon)** | *Fading.* Lies down of his own accord "just for a little while" and cannot be roused without effort. Speaks in short fragments. Says the word *«Σίλας»* once, then forgets he said it. Μίρα is openly crying now. | *«Μίρα... ποια είσαι;»* (he says it quietly, as if asking a question he already knows the answer to and is ashamed of) | **Exhaustion 2.** Medicine DC 13 or any healing spell halts further decline for one hour. **Lay on Hands heals exhaustion at this stage** (per DM ruling — reward the paladin). |
| **Σούρουπο (dusk) — Μίρα's second visit** | *Detaching.* If the party has done nothing, Μίρα arrives at wherever they are, dry-eyed and terrible. *«Έρχεστε; Ή πρέπει να τον κρατήσω μόνη μου τη νύχτα;»* Do not let this moment be easy. This is the last free save. | *«Αν φύγει απόψε, θα πάει μόνος του. Σας παρακαλώ.»* | **Exhaustion 3.** If the party still refuses or delays, mark it on paper. Μίρα's trust is now on a one-way door. |
| **Νύχτα (night)** | *Gone-ish.* Not dead. Not present. Eyes open. Does not speak. Responds to touch but not to names — not even his own. Μίρα sits with him in the dark. | No line. Silence is the line. | **Exhaustion 4.** Magical healing (Lesser Restoration, Greater Restoration, or a full long rest with the compass on the bedside table) will pull him back from here — **once**. A second slide to this stage cannot be reversed. |

**If the party engages**: Each hour they spend with him (compass within 10 ft, set on the table or held nearby) he rolls back one stage toward Πρωί. A full long rest adjacent to the compass restores him to baseline, with a new **permanent trait** — he is the party's first non-compass witness who can *perceive* edits. **This is their reward.**

## Payoff

- **Θωμάς restored to baseline + permanent edit-perception trait** — the party's first non-compass witness. He becomes a quiet long-term ally.
- **Μίρα's total trust** — gives a low-DC bridge to Thread 8 (the Vossos journals).
- **Νεφέλη on the watch for the pregnancy** — she will sense Vanya's attention before anyone else, hours before any visible signs.
- A foreshadow seed: Θωμάς remembers more of the mine than the party found — there is more deeper down (Session 4+).
- Roleplay gold for the bard especially — Θωμάς's broken cadence is a dialogue gift.

## Consequence if ignored

**This is the only thread with real in-world harm for neglect.**

The party has been asked directly for help by a pregnant woman whose husband they already rescued once. If they refuse or deprioritize:

- **By dusk**: Θωμάς is at Exhaustion 3 (see clock). Μίρα comes to find them a second time. If they refuse her again — mark it. She will not trust them with anything ever again.
- **By end of session**: If they never check on him, Θωμάς is at Exhaustion 4. Μίρα is alone with a man who does not know her.
- **Between sessions (if utterly abandoned)**: Θωμάς dies in his sleep. Μίρα miscarries from grief within ten days. The party has lost their first friend in the village — and they were warned. **This is dark fantasy's rule: choices have weight.** Do not apply this lightly; only if the players *actively* abandon Θωμάς after being directly asked twice.
