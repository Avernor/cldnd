*(The Home Base. Vanya is probing Μίρα's baby. Νεφέλη is the early warning. Stakes: a child the party can save, if they're paying attention.)*

The party does NOT need to directly attend to Μίρα this session — Νεφέλη has her. **The full sandbox below is for Session 4 onward, when the party can stay in the village and engage directly.**

If the party returns by dawn (almost certain — the dungeon is a few hours), Μίρα is fine. If they delay past dawn, run **one probe roll** (table below) at advantage to Vanya.

If they delay, Μίρα herself comes to the inn at midmorning, pale and quiet: *«Η Νεφέλη με ξύπνησε στις τέσσερις. Δεν ξέρω τι ξέρει. Αλλά ξέρει.»*

## What's actually happening (DM only)

The real Vanya is now alerted (party dug Sylas's grave in Session 2). She is **probing** Μίρα's pregnancy — the way she probed Sylas before relocating him.

She has not yet committed to an edit. She is testing. *What if this child were never conceived? What if she lost it last week? What if it had been a different child, a different father?*

Each probe is a single touch through the thin spot. Each probe leaves a trace. Νεφέλη — who has delivered every baby in this village for forty years — feels the traces as a *muffled* sense of the pregnancy. The baby is *there.* But the *feeling* of the baby keeps slipping in and out of focus.

She has never felt this before in any pregnancy. She is genuinely afraid.

Στην άλλη γωνία, ο Θωμάς κάθεται όρθιος στην καρέκλα της κουνιστής της — ξύπνιος αλλά ήσυχος, κοιτάζοντας τις φλόγες.

### What Νεφέλη tells them

- *«Η Μίρα είναι γερή. Το μωρό είναι γερό. Αλλά κάτι τα... ψάχνει.»*
- *«Δεν θέλω να σας πω τι σκέφτομαι, γιατί φοβάμαι μη το αλήθευσω λέγοντάς το. Αλλά πρέπει να σας πω: αυτό που έχει η Μίρα δεν θα φύγει μόνο του. Όποιος ή ό,τι την ψάχνει, θα ξανάρθει.»*
- If asked **what does she sense**: *«Σαν δύο φωνές, αλλά μία γυναίκα. Καταλαβαίνετε; Δεν, δεν καταλαβαίνετε. Ούτε εγώ. Είναι σαν να μιλάει κάποια στον εαυτό της — αλλά οι δύο εαυτοί δεν συμφωνούν.»* **(This is Νεφέλη brushing against the fragment vs. real-Vanya boundary. Don't explain it.)**
- *«Η πυξίδα μένει εδώ. Όσο μπορείτε. Όσο μένει η πυξίδα κοντά της, αυτό που σπρώχνει σπρώχνει αλλού.»*

### The compass placement
If they leave the compass with Μίρα: works. The probes deflect. **But they cannot then take it on the road north.** This is the dilemma.

If they take the compass with them: Νεφέλη herself will sit with Μίρα. She is not as good as the compass, but she is something. She will not leave Μίρα's side until they return.

**This is the trade.**

## The Vanya probe roll (DM only)

Roll **once per hour** of in-game time the party is *not* with Μίρα and the compass is *not* with Μίρα.

| d20 | What happens |
|---|---|
| 1-12 | Νothing. Vanya is testing other angles. |
| 13-17 | Probe — the cottage gets cold for a moment. Νεφέλη feels it. Μίρα's belly tightens. Roll again next hour. |
| 18-19 | **Heavy probe** — Μίρα has a sudden contraction. Bleeding. Νεφέλη can stop it but the baby's *feeling* is gone for ~30 minutes after. |
| 20 | **Edit attempted** — Vanya tries to retroactively erase the conception. Without the compass present, this would *succeed* (Mira would forget she was ever pregnant; the village would too). With Νεφέλη in the room, it half-fails: Μίρα keeps the baby but loses two weeks of memory of being pregnant. Confused, frightened, but the baby is alive. **This is your worst-case beat. Use sparingly.** |

**Modifier**: -2 to the roll if they've left the compass at the cottage. Vanya cannot probe through it.

## What the party can do this session

### Stabilize
- Leave the compass at Νεφέλη's during the day → safe but they cannot take it north.
- Leave Νεφέλη at Μίρα's bedside continuously → buys time, doesn't solve.
- Pray, lay on hands, bardic inspiration → Νεφέλη accepts this with quiet gratitude. **Lay on Hands does *something*** — Μίρα sleeps deeper, the probes glance off her for the next hour. Plant this if the Paladin offers.

### Investigate
The party may want to track *who* is doing this. They can't — not from here. The answer is at the tower. **This is the thread that pushes them north.**

### Move Μίρα
If they suggest moving her somewhere else: Νεφέλη says no. *«Όχι σ' αυτή την κατάσταση. Τη μετακινώ και τη χάνω.»* If they push hard, she relents — but Mira loses 2 hours of probe protection in transit, and Vanya gets a roll at advantage.

### Tell Μίρα the truth
If they wake her and explain: she takes it surprisingly well. She has felt the wrongness too. *«Το ξέρω ότι κάτι πάει στραβά. Δεν χρειαζόμουν να μου το πεις. Χρειαζόμουν να μου πεις ότι δεν είμαι τρελή.»* This deepens the trust. **Inspiration to whoever leads the conversation gently.**

## Resolution paths (this session)

### Path A — They leave the compass and go to the tower
- Νεφέλη stays with Μίρα. The compass field protects the cottage at ~70% strength (it is not on a person). One light probe lands but does not take.
- Tower yields major lore (Thread 1).
- Session ends with Μίρα stable, baby alive.
- Νεφέλη is exhausted. The next probe (Session 4) will be harder.

### Path B — They take the compass and leave Νεφέλη
- Roll the probe each hour. Almost certain that one heavy probe lands.
- Μίρα survives but is shaken. Baby still alive at session end (DM keep it that way — kill the baby in Session 4 only if they're being negligent).
- Tower lore acquired.
- Trust with Μίρα and Νεφέλη strained but not broken.

### Path C — They stay all day
- Μίρα and the baby are safe.
- They do not reach the tower.
- The compass continues to grow hotter. Νεφέλη says, quietly, at dusk: *«Πρέπει να πάτε αύριο. Δεν θα κρατήσω άλλη μέρα μόνη.»*
- This is the gentle railroad. Session 4 opens with the journey north.

### Path D — They ignore Νεφέλη's summons
**Don't let this happen** if you can help it. If they truly do, run two heavy probes during the day. Μίρα bleeds. The baby's feeling is gone for hours at a time. Νεφέλη comes to the inn herself by afternoon, furious for the first time in living memory:
> *«Πού είσαστε; Πού είσαστε εσείς; Αυτή η γυναίκα σας περίμενε. Εγώ σας περίμενα. Πάμε. Τώρα.»*

## Foreshadow for Session 4+

If a heavy probe lands and Νεφέλη is in the room, she may catch a fragment of the real Vanya's voice:
> *«Δικό μου το παιδί. Δικό μου ήταν πάντα.»*

Νεφέλη will not understand it but will repeat it to the party, shaken: *«Είπε... κάτι. Νομίζω ότι ήταν "δικό μου το παιδί". Αλλά δεν ξέρω σε ποιο μωρό αναφερόταν. Στο δικό μου; Στης Μίρας; Στο δικό της που έχασε; Δεν ξέρω.»*

**This is the first time the party hears Vanya's *grief* in her own words.** Plant it. It will pay off when they meet her in Session 9.

## Consequence if Thread 2 is fully ignored

- Mira loses the pregnancy mid-Session 4
- Νεφέλη will never trust the party again
- Μίρα and Θωμάς are broken. They survive, but they will not help the party again.

**Make the players feel this if it happens.** Don't be subtle. The dark fantasy rule applies.
