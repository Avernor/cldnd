# Campaign Lore & Session 1 Notes

---

## The Party

### 1. Human Fighter
- 30 years old
- Of noble origin but raised as a blacksmith due to a betrayal of the royal family
- Carries a family sword — may develop abilities later

### 2. Dwarf Paladin
- Lived in the mountains with other dwarves
- His clan was raided by goblins and orcs
- Carries a greataxe that belonged to his family for generations — may develop abilities later

### 3. Gnome Bard
- Raised by an old gnome bard who used and exploited him for money
- Has lived alone since the old bard died
- Makes money and a name for himself on the road

## The Campaign

### Theme
Time travel and mysteries, with humour included.

### The Big Bad — Vanya
- A human woman
- Lost her child
- Is attempting to alter the timeline in order to bring her child back

### Structure
- **Part 1 — Levels 2–5:** Oakhaven (a small village)
- **Part 2 — Levels 5–9:** Kaelen (the capital city)
- **Part 3 — Levels 10+:** Regional

## Session 1

### Starting Point
The three players were already friends. The group was travelling toward Kaelen to make money.

### The Letter
A messenger rider approaches and asks for the bard by name, then hands him a letter.
The letter reads: *"Come to Oakhaven, time is breaking."*
- Written in the bard's own handwriting
- Sealed with the dwarf's clan seal

### The Storm & The Tower
After some travel, a massive unnatural storm forces them to seek shelter. They find an old abandoned wrecked obsidian tower. They find a way into its underground cellar, discovering an old abandoned laboratory.

- They take shelter in the lab
- They find a compass — not a normal compass. Instead of pointing north, it clicks rhythmically like a watch and is warm to the touch
- While in the lab, something happens around them — a kind of field forms (this was an accident; it protects them from timeline changes, though they are unaware of this)

### Arriving at Oakhaven
They travel to Oakhaven and begin asking questions about the letter and the compass. No one knows anything.

### The Inn — Night 1
They spend the night at the inn.
- The barkeep is **Sylas** — a hard and angry man
- They approach a hooded woman writing in a journal (**Vanya**). She is polite but distanced, and leaves.

### Morning — The Missing Husband
A pregnant woman approaches them and asks them to find her husband, who did not return from the lumber mill the previous night with the other workers.

### The Mine
They venture into an old abandoned dwarven mine in the mountains above the lumber mill.
- They free **Thomas** — the pregnant woman's husband
- They find a very old skeleton with a perfectly preserved steel watch. Time moves **backward** in the watch instead of forward.

### The Time Slip
They enter the mine during the day. When they leave, it is night — even though they did not spend the whole day inside.

They return to the inn and ask about the sudden change in time of day. Everyone acts as if everything is normal.

### Reuniting the Couple
They return Thomas to his wife. The party goes to bed.

### The Cliffhanger
They are woken by a loud ticking sound coming from the compass.
- The compass is sizzling to the touch and spinning rapidly
- They go downstairs to find the inn completely different
- Sylas is not there — in his place is an old man
- When asked about Sylas, the old man says they are crazy: Sylas died ten years ago in a barfight
- A rift appears before them, the size of the letter
- The bard begins to disappear from existence
- They take the letter they received and write *"Come to Oakhaven, time is breaking"* — for their past selves to receive
- The session ends
